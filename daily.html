Buddhist Daily Routine

The Buddhist's start their routine at 5am with the morning meeting.
In this they do chanting and silent meditation.
At 6:30am they do their daily cleanup of chores around the main area of the monastery.
At 7:15am they eat breakfast, usually tea and cereal.
At 8:15am they do work. This includes general maintenance of the monastery.
At 10:45am Meal offering (11:30am, daylight saving time).
After the kitchen wash-up, Personal practice - individual meditation and study time.
